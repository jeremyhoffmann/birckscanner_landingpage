Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 20, 2025

Brick Scanner ("we," "our," or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application ("App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

When you use the App, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Photos of LEGO pieces: You can take photos of LEGO pieces with the App. These photos are processed to identify the pieces and suggest possible LEGO sets. Some photos may also be used to improve and train our machine learning models.
  • Device information: Such as device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage data: Such as IP address, app interactions, and general analytics data (e.g., frequency of use, crash logs).

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or contact details. No user account is required to use the App.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide the core functionality of the App (scanning LEGO pieces and suggesting sets).
  • To improve and optimize our machine learning models and recognition accuracy.
  • To monitor performance, fix issues, and improve user experience.
  • To generate anonymous analytics that help us understand how the App is used.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your information.

We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • With trusted third-party service providers (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics services) who help us operate and improve the App.
  • If required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • To protect our rights, safety, or property, or that of other users.

4. Data Retention

  • Photos taken in the App are processed and may be stored temporarily or used to improve our machine learning models.
  • Analytics and technical data are retained only as long as necessary for the purposes described above.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child, please contact us at contact@brick-scanner.com, and we will delete it.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your data, such as the right to request access, correction, or deletion. You can contact us at contact@brick-scanner.com to exercise these rights.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify users by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: contact@brick-scanner.com

By using Brick Scanner, you accept this privacy policy.
